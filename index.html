Who We Are

Built on a Mission to Sustain Life Through Water

Pishon Engineering Ltd is a Uganda-registered engineering firm specialising in Water, Sanitation, and Hygiene (WASH) infrastructure. We partner with governments, NGOs, schools, health facilities, and communities to design and deliver water systems that work — and keep working.

Our name draws from the Pishon River, one of the four rivers of Eden — a symbol of life, abundance, and the foundational role water plays in every human community.

Timely Delivery

How We Work

A Rigorous, Community-Centred Approach

Every project follows a structured methodology that puts technical excellence and community ownership at the centre.

1
Site Assessment & Survey
We conduct hydrogeological surveys, geophysical investigations, and community needs assessments to determine the most appropriate water source and system design.
2
Engineering Design & Bill of Quantities
Our engineers develop detailed technical designs, specifications, and accurate cost estimates aligned with client budgets and donor requirements.
3
Construction & Installation
Supervised construction by qualified field teams using certified materials. Regular progress reporting keeps stakeholders informed at every stage.
4
Testing, Commissioning & Handover
Systems are rigorously tested, water quality verified, and communities trained in operation and basic maintenance before formal handover.
5
Aftercare & Maintenance Support
We offer ongoing maintenance contracts and rapid-response support to ensure systems remain functional long after project closure.
